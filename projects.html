The SAGE System

My senior project at Western Washington University, this was started and mostly completed over the course of a one year product development cycle based loosely off JPL and IEEE's project development guidelines, with some influences from SCRUM thrown in. The end goal was a Semi-Autonomous Growing Environment that was modularized, 3D printed, low-cost, and accessible to users in apartment or dormitory style housing situations. It was very much a learning experience and while the functional prototype was legitimately functional, it ended up only having about 75% of its intended features due to time constraints - because choosing a senior project with mechanical and electrical components when you're only supposed to focus on electrical stuff is always a recipe for, if not disaster, then certainly missed deadlines.

Data Miner Idle Game

Currently in alpha development, soon to be in public beta, very much a one person project at the moment - but I'd be more than happy to take contributions or support in any form, if these kinds of things strike your fancy.
