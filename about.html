Who Am I? (A Brief Bio)

Born in '94, I grew up as electronics rapidly grew smaller, more sophisticated, and all of society began to change from the relentless adoption of these new technologies. This inevitably led to an interest in learning how all these things worked, which led to an interest in programming and ripping electronics apart, and one thing led to another and, well, here's some of the end results. In addition to electronics, I dabble in 3D Printing & Modeling, metal crafting, leather working, writing, and botany - often times trying to find new or interesting ways to combine one or more. For questions, comments, or anything in between, feel free to reach out via email or whatever your preferred medium is and I'll be happy to talk!
